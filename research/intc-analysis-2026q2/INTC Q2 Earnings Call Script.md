Key facts from the call:
- Q2 2026 revenue $16.1B (+25% YoY), $1.8B above guidance midpoint; non-GAAP EPS $0.42 vs $0.20 guided; non-GAAP gross margin 41.8% (+280bps vs guide).
- 7th consecutive quarter of beating guidance; strongest revenue growth in 15+ years.
- Raising FY2026 CapEx guidance to >$20B; FY2027 CapEx guided "significantly above" FY2026, majority US-based.
- Intel 18A in high-volume production (Panther Lake, Wildcat Lake); 18A-P entered risk production; Intel 14A PDK 0.9 on track for October 2026; 14A risk production 2H27, committed to HVM in 2028.
- Server CPU demand outlook raised again; Xeon 6+/Clearwater Forest (first 18A server product) launched.
- ASIC/purpose-built silicon run-rate ~$2B now, targeting ~$4B; Fortinet Security Processor 6 collaboration announced.
- Q3 2026 guidance: revenue $15.8–16.8B, non-GAAP gross margin ~42%, non-GAAP EPS $0.38.
