## Capital markets activity this quarter (new this release — not present in Q1)
- **Equity raise (June 2026):** Class A + Class C common stock + mandatory convertible preferred stock issued for aggregate **net proceeds of $49.6B**, earmarked for general corporate purposes incl. CapEx to scale AI infrastructure/global compute.
- **ATM program:** entered an equity distribution agreement to sell up to **$40.0B** of Class A/C stock over time; proceeds primarily intended to cover tax obligations on employee equity grants (SBC-related), not general funding; **zero shares sold under the ATM as of Jun 30, 2026**.
- **Senior unsecured notes (Q2 2026):** issued for **net proceeds of $20.3B**, general corporate purposes.
- **Preferred dividend:** Board declared $12.15/share quarterly cash dividend on Series A/B 6.25% mandatory convertible preferred stock (≈$0.60/depositary share), payable Aug 15, 2026. Common dividend $0.22/share, payable Sep 14, 2026.
- 19 shares of mandatory convertible preferred stock issued and outstanding as of Jun 30, 2026 (0 at Dec 31, 2025), liquidation preference $1,000/share, carried at $18,023M incl. APIC.
