## Meta / OpenAI Warrants

- Meta: warrant for up to 160M shares at $0.01 strike, vesting on GPU purchase milestones AND stock-price targets, through February 23, 2031. Tied to the expanded partnership to deploy up to 6GW of AMD Instinct GPUs (custom MI450-based accelerator + Helios rack-scale with EPYC Venice).
- OpenAI: warrant for up to 160M shares at $0.01 strike, issued October 2025, same vesting logic (GPU milestones + stock performance).
- Combined potential dilution: 320M shares (~20% of ~1.63B shares outstanding).
- Zero shares vested under either warrant as of March 28, 2026.
