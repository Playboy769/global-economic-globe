## Notes for cross-referencing against the call

- Q4 royalty growth (+11% Y/Y) is the softest metric on the print relative to the 20%+ headline revenue growth — CFO attributed this to a tough prior-year comp (MediaTek Snapdragon 400 ramp in Q4 FY25) and guided royalty growth back to ~20% Y/Y in Q1 FY27.
- RPO declined 7% Y/Y ($2,071m vs $2,226m) even as ACV grew 22% Y/Y — management attributed this to "improvements in the timing of revenue conversion," i.e., backlog converting to recognized revenue faster than new commitments are being added to backlog. Worth reconciling against the Financials.html valuation tab.
- Share-based compensation cash cost is large and rising: $1,052m equity-settled SBC (FY26) vs $820m (FY25), plus $529m of cash paid for withholding tax on vested shares (FY26) vs $120m (FY25) — this is a meaningful and growing cash outflow in financing activities, not just a GAAP/non-GAAP add-back.
- Non-GAAP FCF of $882m (FY26) vs GAAP operating cash flow of $1,524m — the ~$642m gap is almost entirely PP&E capex ($545m FY26 vs $219m FY25, +149% Y/Y), consistent with management's commentary about "increased investment in PP&E" ahead of the AGI CPU silicon ramp.
