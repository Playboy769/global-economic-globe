## The GAAP-vs-non-GAAP gap the call never named

The call's opening disclaimer states "all financial numbers we talk about today, other than revenue, will be on an adjusted non-GAAP basis" — but every subsequent reference to operating income, net income, and EPS was stated as a bare number with no "non-GAAP" qualifier attached in the sentence itself (e.g., Matthew Prince: "We delivered an operating profit of $73.1 million, representing an operating margin of 11.4%"; Thomas Seifert: "Our net income in the quarter was $94 million or diluted net income per share of $0.25").

- Non-GAAP operating income cited on call: **$73.1M** (11.4% margin) vs GAAP operating **loss** of $(62.0)M — a $135.1M gap
- Non-GAAP net income cited on call: **$94M** ($0.25/share) vs GAAP net **loss** of $(22.9)M — a $116.9M gap
- The largest single reconciling item is stock-based compensation: **$114.2M** in Q1 2026 (vs $95.5M Q1 2025, +19.6% YoY), broken down by function:
  - Cost of revenue: $3.7M
  - Sales & Marketing: $39.3M
  - R&D: $43.0M
  - G&A: $28.2M
- SBC alone (5.9% of revenue) does not fully bridge the ~$135M operating-income gap, implying additional add-backs (payroll tax on SBC, amortization of acquired intangibles, and similar items typical of Cloudflare's non-GAAP reconciliation) not itemized in the press release excerpts reviewed.

## Remaining Performance Obligations & Deferred Revenue

## Restructuring Plan (announced same-day, May 7, 2026 8-K — not yet reflected in Q1 balance sheet)

- ~20% workforce reduction (~1,100 positions), "across all functions and geographies" except quota-carrying AE capacity
- Total estimated charges: **$140M-$150M** for FY2026
  - Cash (severance, notice period, benefits): $105M-$110M
  - Non-cash (accelerated vesting of share-based awards): $35M-$40M
- Majority of charges expected in **Q2 2026**; plan execution "substantially complete" by end of Q3 2026
- **No restructuring accrual was recorded as of March 31, 2026** — the entire charge is a subsequent event relative to the quarter being reported, meaning none of Q1's GAAP loss is attributable to the layoffs themselves; the GAAP loss is driven by ongoing SBC and opex load layered on a business that is only 11.4% operating-margin-positive on a non-GAAP basis.
- Headcount at quarter-end: ~5,500 (pre-reduction)
