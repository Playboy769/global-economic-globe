**⚑ Quality-of-earnings flag:** Net income +81% is heavily inflated by OI&E. Cash-flow statement shows **–$36,804M "gain on debt and equity securities, net"** — i.e. ~$36.8B of the $37.7B OI&E is investment gains, overwhelmingly **unrealized gains on the non-marketable equity portfolio** (which jumped +$38.3B on the balance sheet). Roughly tax-affecting the $36.8B gain (~19%) implies "core" net income of ~$32–33B and core diluted EPS of ~$2.6–2.7 — the reported $5.11 roughly doubles the operating earnings power.
