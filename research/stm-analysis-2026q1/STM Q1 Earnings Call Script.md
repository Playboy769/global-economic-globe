Apr 23, 2026, 3:30 AM EDT — Powered by Quartr

## Summary

STM delivered Q1 2026 net revenues of $3.1B (up YoY, above guidance midpoint), with gross margin at 33.8% and non-GAAP EPS of $0.13. Management guided for Q2 revenues of $3.45B (+11.6% QoQ, +24.9% YoY) and gross margin of 34.8%, citing strong AI, data center, and automotive momentum, while reiterating double-digit full-year growth and improved margin trajectory.
