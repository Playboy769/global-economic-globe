Call date: April 22, 2026, 5:00 PM EDT

## Summary

Lam Research delivered record March quarter results with revenue of $5.84B (up 24% YoY, 9% QoQ) and EPS of $1.47, both above guidance. Management raised 2026 WFE outlook to $140B, guided for June quarter revenue of $6.6B ±$400M and record EPS of $1.65 ±$0.15, citing accelerating AI-driven demand and strong momentum across NAND, DRAM, and advanced packaging.
